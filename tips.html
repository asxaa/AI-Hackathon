Warm-Up Properly

Before any training session, spend 10-15 minutes warming up with light cardio and dynamic stretches to prevent injury.

Focus on Form

Proper technique is key to progress and injury prevention. Take time to learn correct movements for your sport.

Stay Hydrated

Drink water before, during, and after exercise to maintain optimal performance and recovery.

Rest and Recover

Allow your body time to recover with adequate sleep and rest days to avoid burnout.
